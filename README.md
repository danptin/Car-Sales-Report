# 🚗 Car Sales Dashboard | Power BI Project

## 📊 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Car Sales Dashboard built using Microsoft Power BI** to analyze and visualize sales performance of a car dealership company.  

The dashboard provides insights into key business metrics such as **Total Sales, Average Price, Cars Sold, Regional Performance, and Customer Preferences**.

It helps stakeholders track performance trends and make **data-driven business decisions**.

---

# 🎯 Project Objective
The objective of this project is to develop a **dynamic Power BI dashboard** that:

- Tracks sales performance over time
- Analyzes trends in car sales
- Identifies growth opportunities
- Provides clear visualization of key business metrics

---

# ❗ Problem Statement
The company sells various car models across multiple regions but lacks an effective analytical tool to monitor performance.

Without proper data visualization it becomes difficult to:

- Monitor **sales performance**
- Compare **current vs previous year sales**
- Identify **customer preferences**
- Evaluate **regional dealer performance**

To solve this, a **Power BI dashboard** was developed to provide interactive and real-time insights.

---

# 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Sales Overview
- YTD Total Sales
- MTD Total Sales
- YOY Growth in Sales
- Difference between YTD and Previous YTD Sales

### Average Price Analysis
- YTD Average Price
- MTD Average Price
- YOY Growth in Average Price
- Difference between YTD and Previous YTD Average Price

### Cars Sold Metrics
- YTD Cars Sold
- MTD Cars Sold
- YOY Growth in Cars Sold
- Difference between YTD and Previous YTD Cars Sold

---

# 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

### 1️⃣ Weekly Sales Trend
Line chart showing **weekly sales performance across the year**.

### 2️⃣ Sales by Body Style
Pie chart showing distribution of sales by:
- SUV
- Hatchback
- Sedan
- Passenger
- Hardtop

### 3️⃣ Sales by Car Color
Pie chart displaying which colors customers prefer.

### 4️⃣ Sales by Dealer Region
Map visualization showing **regional sales distribution**.

### 5️⃣ Company-wise Sales Trend
Table comparing sales across different manufacturers such as:
- Volvo
- Volkswagen
- Toyota
- Subaru
- Porsche

### 6️⃣ Detailed Sales Data
Grid displaying complete sales records including:

- Car Model
- Body Style
- Color
- Sales Amount
- Dealer Region
- Date
